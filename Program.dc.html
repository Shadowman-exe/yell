No theory-only weeks

What you actually do.

Seven steps from idea to revenue. Every week you ship something real.

STEP 01

Choose your product

Pick a product you believe in and build an entire business around it.

STEP 02

Source from wholesale markets

Visit Sadar Bazaar and Chawri Bazaar. Walk the lanes. Find your supplier.

STEP 03

Negotiate like a buyer

Haggle on prices, lock unit economics, and learn margins the hard way.

STEP 04

Build your brand

Name it. Design the logo. Create packaging. Make it look like it belongs on a shelf.

STEP 05

Launch your store

Set up an online storefront and a physical pop-up — sell on both channels simultaneously.

STEP 06

Sell and keep the margin

Real customers. Real transactions. Real revenue you take home.

STEP 07

Pitch on Demo Day

Present your venture to a panel of real operators and judges. Defend every decision you made.
