About YELL

We believe every student deserves to build something of their own.

Why YELL exists.

There's a gap between studying business and running one. Students spend years on case studies and exams, then graduate having never sourced a product, closed a sale, or defended a margin.

YELL closes that gap. We put students in the market with real money, real customers, and real stakes - so they graduate as founders, not just graduates.

Vision

A generation of execution-driven entrepreneurial thinkers.

Mission

To build structured business-building environments inside universities.
